The Pills:

Below are the pills that are recommended/works best for people with a BMI of below 16.5 & 18.5, and between 18.5-24.9

1. My Choice

The My Choice pill is NOT to be used on a regular basis. This medication is used as an emergency contraceptive or backup in case birth control fails or is used incorrectly. Any women or girl of reproductive age may need emergency contraception to avoid unwanted pregnancy.
